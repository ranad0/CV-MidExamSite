❓ Help — How to Use This Site

Best Study Order

  1. Start with 🟥 Real Exam — these are the actual past exam questions and highest priority.
  2. Move to 🔥 Very Likely — these are repeated lecture patterns and instructor-highlighted topics.
  3. Use 🧠 ALL Flashcards by topic to cover everything cleanly.
  4. Practice ✍️ Blanks to test memory of key terms and traps.
  5. Read 📌 Important Ideas + 🚨 Warnings before the exam for high-yield revision.
  6. Finish with 🧪 Exam Simulator to practice answering under time pressure.

Buttons Explained

  • Mark as Reviewed: click after you understand a card. It turns green and counts your progress.
  • Only Unreviewed: hides cards you already marked, so you focus only on what remains.
  • Quick Revision: shows Real Exam + Very Likely questions together.
  • Search: type any keyword like “VGG”, “pooling”, “motion”, or “segmentation”.
